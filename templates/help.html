Help navigating through the system

How to create an account?

Once you open the app,a login form appears.Click on the sign up link which will direct you to a registering form.Enter your details as required the click register button.

How to login?

Once you open the app,a login form appears.Enter your details according to how you had previously registered.Then login.

How to add a new product

Once you have logged in,you are directed to a dashboard.On the side navigation,you will be able to see links directing you to various pages.Click on the products link.Click on add new product and insert the required information.Submit the information.
